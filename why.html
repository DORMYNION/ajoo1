Why Ajoo Platform?

  • Are you struggling to meet your savings and personal finance goals?

  • Do you need money for that start-up idea, school bills or rent, dream car or even vacation?

  • Have you been in a fix or caught up in the throes of a project, and then wish you had enough money to sort yourself out without the hassles of getting loans on interest?

It is obvious that saving money by yourself for a specific goals can be quite challenging and dauting, especially with the ongoing economic crunch. So, doing it as a team is the new cool, as it not only encourages your saving habit but also refuels your commitment. This is why Ajoo platform is here to help turn your dreams into financial goals by providing an online tool that connects you to other like-minded people thereby making saving easier, mutually beneficial and fun for all .
